About me

I am Ahmad Hijazi, a Mechanical Engineer who is currently pursuing my Master's Degree at the Lebanese American University. I graduated with Distinction from the Lebanese American University in 2021 with a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering.

My research interests include renewable energy technologies especially Wind Turbines, Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and Numerical Simulations. My current work includes modelling and simulating a vertical axis wind turbine and investigating the effect of modifying the turbine blade on the performance of the turbine.
